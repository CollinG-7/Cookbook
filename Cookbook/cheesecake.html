Collin's Basic Cheesecake

Ingredients


  • CRUST
  • 230 grams graham cracker crumbs
  • 6 Tablespoons melted butter
  • 3 Tablespoons Sugar

Mix thoroughly
Put in well buttered spring form pan
Bake at 350 about 10 mins, careful not to burn, let cool.

  • FILLING
  • 3 eight oz packages of cream cheese
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1 Tablespoon Vanilla

Cream the above ingredients together in the order listed.
Bake at 325 degrees farenheit about one hour, or until done.
