Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of Guanciale (or bacon)
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 egg yolks
  • 4 ounces of freshly grated cheese (ideally half Pecorino Romano and Parmigiano Reggiano)
  • Ground black pepper
  • Salt
  • 16 ounces of pasta

Directions

  • Chop the quanciale or bacon into small pieces
  • Combine the eggs, egg yolk, cheese, and pepper in a bowl
  • Begin boiling your water and cooking your guanciale, you should aim to have the pasta almost done just about when the guanciale will be done cooking
  • When the guanciale is done, kill the heat and add your pasta and a splash or two to the pasta cooking water
  • Toss the pasta in the fat from the guanciale thoroughly, then add the egg and cheese mixture and move everything around vigourously
  • The sauce should thicken, if not the desired consistency, add some pasta cooking water. Season to taste with salt
