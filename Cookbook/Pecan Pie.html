Pecan Pie

Ingredient List


  • 2 cups pecans
  • 1 pie crust
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup dark corn syrup
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • a splash of maple syrup
  • a dash of cinnamon

Directions


  • Preheat ooven to 350 degrees F
  • Roll out your pie crust and arrange in your pie tin, make sure to allow to refidgerate at least 30 mins after shaping and before baking
  • Combine eggs, corn syrup, sugar, salt, butter, vanilla, cornstarch(mixed with a splash of water), maple syrup, and cinnamon in a large bowl
  • chop most of the pecans and pour into pie crust, arrange the remainder of whole pecans on top in a pattern if desired
  • Bake at 350 degrees for about an hour or until filling is set, will likely need to cover the pie and crust with aluminum foil to prevent burning
