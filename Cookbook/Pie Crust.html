Pie Crust

Ingredients


  • 2 & 1/2 Cups Flour
  • 2 sticks or 8 oz butter, cold
  • 2 Tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon salt
  • 4-8 Tablespoons of 1/2 water, 1/2 vodka, cold

Directions

  • Mix flour, salt, and sugar in a large bowl.
  • Cut butter into small cubes.
  • Blend butter into flour mixture either with a pastry cutter or your hands, do not let the butter get melty, but break it down so that the butter is throughly incorporated.
  • Add a few tablespoons of the vodka water mix until the right consistancy is achieved.
  • Put the dough in the fridge at least 30 minuntes before rolling out for use.
